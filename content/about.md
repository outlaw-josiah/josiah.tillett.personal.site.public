Josiah is a skilled IT Professional from the Baltimore/Washington DC Area who specializes in DevOps, incident response, and workflow analysis. His work experience has given him a love of problem solving and engaging with unique situations. He has a strong background in open-source, and enjoys working in fast-paced environments with rapidly changing needs. In his day job, Josiah is a member of [Adaptavist's](https://adaptavist.com) North American DevOps consulting team, where he spends his days helping clients revamp their development practices and build robust automation to support their strategies.

He also spends the majority of his free time volunteering as a Volunteer Coordinator at [MAGFest Inc.](https://super.magfest.org/what-is-magfest), a 501(c)3 non-profit based out of Baltimore, Maryland. Josiah is responsible for drafting policies, staking out cultural standards, and being a core line of support to MAGFest’s 2,000 volunteers. Josiah also helps guide MAGFest's IT policy and infrastructure to support their multiple annual events.


When not volunteering with MAGFest, Josiah also runs JAT Audio, his A/V production business. JAT Audio is focused primarily on live music and events, with an emphasis on weddings, conferences, and rock shows. JAT Audio supports VGM events such as MAGFest and VGMCon through both rental and labor donations.
